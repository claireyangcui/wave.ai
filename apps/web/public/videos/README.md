# Video Files for Genre Backgrounds

Place your background videos here with the following names:

1. **neon-house.mp4** - High-energy visuals (neon lights, city scenes, club visuals)
2. **lo-fi-drift.mp4** - Chill/relaxed visuals (waves, clouds, rain, cozy scenes)
3. **industrial-tech.mp4** - Dark/mechanical visuals (factories, machines, abstract tech)

## Video Recommendations

- **Format**: MP4 (H.264 codec for best browser support)
- **Resolution**: 1920x1080 or higher
- **Duration**: 10-30 seconds (they loop automatically)
- **File size**: Keep under 10MB for fast loading
- **Style**: Abstract, ambient, or atmospheric - no faces or text
